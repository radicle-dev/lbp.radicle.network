Q1: What is the purpose of this event?

The purpose of the proposed Liquidity Bootstrapping event is to:

  1. Distribute the Radicle token among the wider Radicle community, beyond early contributors and team members, to decentralize decision-making power.
  2. Build deep liquidity for the Radicle network, setting it up for long-term success.
  3. Begin price discovery for the Radicle token.

Q2: What is a Liquidity Bootstrapping Pool?

Balancer is a decentralized exchange that allows anyone to swap one token for another. It‚Äôs built entirely on smart contracts, allowing transactions to take place securely and without the need for an intermediary.

Balancer‚Äôs Liquidity Bootstrapping Pool (LBP) is a Balancer pool that automatically adjusts its pool weights over time. This applies downward price pressure on the token and disincentivizes front-running, speculation and whale buying. See Balancer‚Äôs Liquidity Bootstrapping Pool FAQ for more information.

Q3: Why was this specific mechanism (LBP) chosen?

The LBP has a number of properties that are desired for a liquidity bootstrapping event:

  1. It prioritizes humans over bots
  2. It deals with front-running well
  3. It optimizes for smaller orders instead of ‚Äúwhale buying‚Äù
  4. The distribution is permissionless and scalable

This mechanism has also been battle tested by a number of teams like Hydra, APY Finance, and Perpetual protocol.

Q9: How was the initial price calculated?

The price was calculated based on the amounts of tokens in the Balancer pool and their weights:

3 750 000 RAD and 3 500 000 USDC in weights 92.5 : 7.5 = (3500000*92.5)/(3750000*7.5) = 11.5 USDC per RAD
